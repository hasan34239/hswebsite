  • Help, I'm not sure what type of package or services I need!

    Not to fear! Just let us know your website goals, and we can provide options and quotes for the services that would best suit your needs.

  • What are the advantages of a web design package?

    Our design team can help you get your ideas off the ground, so you can focus on your business. Whether you are looking for a little bit of help, or want an entirely hands-off website development experience, our team can cater to your needs so you can feel confident with your website. The best part? Your design will be built on the Website.com site builder, so you can maintain your website after launch, saving you time and resources.

  • Can I offer design feedback?

    Of course! We want to make sure you're in love with your design. Our designers will keep you updated with every step of the process. Our web design packages include 2 revisions and drafts to make sure you're happy with your website.

  • Do I have control of my website after launch?

    One of the best part of our design services is that you have total control of your website after launch! Your site will be built on our Website.com drag and drop site builder, so all you'll need is a regular, premium Website.com plan to easily add updates at no additional design cost. If you'd rather leave everything to us, not to worry! After launch, we're here to offer additional services at an affordable rate.

  • What content do I need to provide?

    If you are not looking for content writing services, you can provide our team with all the text needed for your website. You can also provide any of your own images or logos that you would like to include - however, if you don't have any images, it's not a problem! Our team can source copyright-free images from our image library.

  • Can you help with my mobile design?

    Having a mobile-friendly design is crucial for reaching clients and for optimizing your rankings on search engines. We can create a mobile version of your design at a reasonable, add-on price. Note that we do not offer mobile design services as a standalone package.

  • Why should I choose Website.com web design?

    One of the key advantages you'll love is having your website design, hosting and services all in one place. By having full knowledge of your website infrastructure, our team is able to take your website from idea to launch in the most efficient way possible - with no time lost searching for the proper communication channels. By having everything in one place, it also means you have a variety of service options to choose from as your website grows. Best of all, you can count on our amazing customer support team to be here for you, now and in the future.

  • What are maintenance fees after my site goes live?

    Unlike other web design agencies, you have total control of your website after your design is delivered! That means all you'll need is the regular, premium Website.com subscription plan to keep your site running - no extra design maintenance fees are required. If you want to make future updates, feel free to log into your site builder account, and make tweaks on your own at no added cost. Or if you prefer, our team is here for you for additional design services at an affordable rate.
